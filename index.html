Guidelines for prevention from COVID-19


Cover mouth while coughing

Cover your mouth with a cloth while coughing or sneezing.
Else, use your elbow to cough or sneeze.

Use a mask when outdoors

Use a 3-layered mask if :

  • you are coughing, sneezing or have fever.
  • you are close to a patient
  • you are going outdoors.
  • you are living in a crowded house.

Wash hands regularly

Try to use hand gloves.
Wash your hands after :

  • coughing or sneezing.
  • eating.
  • coming home from outside.
  • touching other people.
  • touching pet animals.

Avoid contact with animals

Some coronaviruses that infect animals have become able to infect humans and then spread between people.

Dont socialise

Avoid interaction with other people.
Try to maintain at least 1 metre distance from other people.
Try to lead an isolated routine as far as possible.

Dont touch your face with dirty hands

The virus can live on metal and plastic for days, so simply adjusting your eyeglasses with unwashed hands may be enough to infect yourself.
